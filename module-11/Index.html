About Our School

A Legacy of
Excellence Since 2005

Founded with a vision to bridge the gap between quality education and accessibility, Bright Future has grown into one of Anambra's most respected secondary schools.

  • Accredited by the Anambra State Ministry of Education
  • Over 2,000 successful alumni in top Nigerian universities
  • Dedicated guidance and counselling services
  • Scholarship programmes for outstanding students
  • Regular educational excursions and STEM workshops

Applications are open for the 2026/2027 academic session. Follow the simple steps below to secure your child's place.

1

Submit Application Form

Fill out the online application form or collect a physical form at the school office. No application fee required.

2

Entrance Examination

Candidates sit a qualifying exam in English and Mathematics. Date communicated after form submission.

3

Interview & Assessment

Shortlisted candidates and parents attend a brief interview and orientation session.

Offer & Enrolment
